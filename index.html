NextUp is an on-demand services marketplace that connects you with nearby professionals across 14 categories — from beauty and home cleaning to tech support and pet services. Think of it as one app for everything you need done.

When you need a service, you create a broadcast — a short request describing what you need, when, and where. Nearby providers who match your service category see your request and can respond with their availability and price. You choose the best fit.

NextUp is completely free for customers. For service providers, the first 3 months are free — no card required to start. After that, plans are $19/month or $199/year (save over 15%). No commissions, no hidden fees. Cancel anytime.
