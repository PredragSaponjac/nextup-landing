FAQ

Questions? Answered.

NextUp is an on-demand services marketplace that connects you with nearby professionals across 14 categories — from beauty and home cleaning to tech support and pet services. Think of it as one app for everything you need done.

When you need a service, you create a broadcast — a short request describing what you need, when, and where. Nearby providers who match your service category see your request and can respond with their availability and price. You choose the best fit.

NextUp is completely free for customers. For service providers, plans start at $19/month or $199/year (save over 15%). No commissions, no hidden fees. Cancel anytime.

We offer 113 services across 14 categories: Beauty & Personal Care, Spa & Wellness, Health & Fitness, Home Cleaning, Home Maintenance & Repair, Lawn & Outdoor, Pet Services, Childcare & Family, Senior & Elder Care, Moving & Delivery, Automotive, Tech & Smart Home, Events & Entertainment, and Laundry & Clothing.

Download the NextUp app, select "I'm a Provider," and complete your profile. Choose your service categories, set your availability and service area, and you're ready to start receiving customer broadcasts. Plans start at just $19/month.

Yes. NextUp is available on iOS and Android. The mobile app is the primary way to use NextUp — both for finding services and for providers managing their business on the go.

Payments are handled directly between you and the provider. NextUp doesn't take a cut of your transaction. Providers set their own prices, and customers pay however they and the provider agree — cash, card, Venmo, or any method that works for both parties.

Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the app settings. Your account stays active until the end of your billing period.
